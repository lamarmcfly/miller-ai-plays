## Email body

Hi everyone,

Most of you are already using AI tools. Most of you are also using them like Google — which is a waste of what they can do.

Academic Enrichment Services and three of your classmates spent the last few weeks building **Miller AI Plays** — a library of short, peer-tested AI workflows that take 90 seconds to learn and 5 minutes to use. No long guides. No theory. Just specific, repeatable workflows for things you do every week: Qbank error review, lecture-to-Anki conversion, shelf prep, OSCE practice, and more.

**It's live now: [SharePoint URL]**

A few that are ready today:

- **Turn Wrong Qbank Questions Into a Study Plan** — paste your wrongs, get back what category of error you made and one targeted next step
- **Lecture Notes → Anki Cards in 5 Minutes** — drop in your lecture notes, get back board-style Anki cards before you close your laptop
- **Make a Concept Stick with a Socratic Tutor** — when re-reading First Aid isn't working, this forces you to reason through the concept instead
- **Build a Shelf-Ready Notebook in NotebookLM** — curated source notebook for any shelf, in under 10 minutes
- **Run an OSCE Encounter Simulation** — practice patient encounters with realistic pushback before the real thing

Plus three more authored by your peers on the founding Miller AI Plays Council:
- [Council Play 1 + author cohort]
- [Council Play 2 + author cohort]
- [Council Play 3 + author cohort]

**A few things to know:**

- Every Play works on the free tier of the tools we recommend. You don't need to pay for anything.
- Plays open in whatever AI tool they use (Claude, Copilot, NotebookLM). We don't proxy or store any of your work.
- Each Play has native comments — tell us what's broken or missing.
- The library is run by the Miller AI Plays Council, a small group of your classmates. Applications for next year's Council open in spring.

This is built by Miller students for Miller students. If you've got a workflow that's working for you and isn't in the library yet, submit it. Submission instructions are on the site.

Open it on your phone. Pick one. Use it tonight.

[**Open Miller AI Plays →**](https://[your-sharepoint-url])

— Lamar Martin
Associate Director, Academic Enrichment Services
Director, Medical Student Disability Services
Miller School of Medicine
